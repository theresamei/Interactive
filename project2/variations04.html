P A N C A K E ?
by Shel Silverstein








                                                      Who wants a pancake,
                                                                                                                                          Sweet and piping hot?
                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Good little Grace looks up and says,
                                                      "I'll take the one on top."
                                                                                                                                          Who else wants a pancake,
                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Fresh off the griddle?



                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Terrible Theresa smiles and says,
                                                                                                                                                                                                                        "I'll take the one in the middle."
